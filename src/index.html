Our stories

Denny & Julia

together since 2019

We love happy ends!

At 62 years young, if anyone had told me I would be in love for the second time in my life, I would have disagreed. After I moved to Charleston in June 2017 as a widow in order to be closer to family, I knew I needed to find a companion; I found myself talking to myself most of the time. I told all of my friends that I was looking for a dog. I may have been alone before, but Donald has filled the hole I had in my heart and even though he doesn’t answer back I now have someone to talk to, anytime I want.
